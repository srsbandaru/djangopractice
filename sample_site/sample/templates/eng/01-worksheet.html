A group of friends are discussing plans for a party. Complete the dialogue with suitable pronouns from the box. You will need to use some of these Pronouns more than once.

him her they he our mine I she you we us it

Nadia: Bhim, let's organise a birthday party for Geeta next Sunday. What do you think?
Bhim: I don't mind. But it was Raja who first suggested this, so, let us ask him.
Nadia: Who all should I invite?
Bhim: Ravi won't be here. He is going to lucknow to see his aunt.
Nadia: What about the food and cake? should we bring cooked food from home or should we buy it from the market?
Bhim: I think that will be too expensive. Let each one of our bring one item.
Nadia: Who do you think can arrange a music system for playing music? I would have brought mine but has gone for repair.
Bhim: Don't worry. My neighbours have one, they are nice people. If I request them, they would surely lend it to me.
Nadia: That's good. Now coming back to our list, who else should I invite?
Bhim: I think we can invite Rose who joined our school last week. She seems to be a friendly girl.
Nadia: Okay, so I will invite her. What about Sanjay? He gets along well with all of us. He will entertain and make us laugh too.
Bhim: Let's ask Rose and Sanjay if they would like to join our party. It will be nice if they agree.
