Orange Squash

How do you prepare Orange Squash?

  • Select juicy oranges and extract the juice.
  • Take water, sugar and citric acid and boil the mixture till the sugar is completely dissolved.
  • Add orange colour essense and juice.
  • Dissolved the potassium metabisulphate in a little juice and mix it into the prepared squash.
  • Pour into sterilized bottles to cool.
  • Seal it or close the bottle tightly store the bottle in a cool place away from the sun.
